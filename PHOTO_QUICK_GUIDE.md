## ðŸŽ¨ How to Use the New Features

### 1. Upload a Photo
- Click "Upload New Photo" button
- Select one or more photos (JPG/PNG, up to 4MB each)
- Photos appear in the gallery

### 2. Select & Show Photo
- Click on a photo in the gallery to select it
- Toggle "Show Photo" to display it on your CV

### 3. Choose Photo Shape
- **Circle**: Professional headshot look (recommended)
- **Square**: Modern, clean appearance
- **Rounded**: Subtle rounded corners

### 4. Set Photo Size (NEW!)
- Drag the "Photo Size" slider
- Range: 40px (small) to 120px (large)
- Default: 60px for sidebar, 80px for center

### 5. Position Your Photo
**Two Ways**:
- **9-Point Grid**: Click on a grid position (Top-Left, Center, Bottom-Right, etc.)
- **Fine-Tune Sliders**: Adjust Horizontal (0-100%) and Vertical (0-100%)

### 6. Customize Border (NEW!)
**Border Color**:
- Click the color square to open color picker
- Or enter a hex code manually (e.g., `#FF0000` for red)

**Border Width**:
- Drag the "Border Width" slider
- Range: 0px (no border) to 8px (thick border)
- Preview updates in real-time

### 7. Position on CV
Choose where the photo appears:
- **Left**: Sidebar (left side)
- **Right**: Sidebar (right side)
- **Center**: Top of CV (header)
- **None**: Hide photo

## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### For Best Results
- âœ… Use professional headshots
- âœ… Ensure good lighting in photo
- âœ… Center your face for portraits
- âœ… Use circle shape for traditional CVs
- âœ… Keep border width subtle (2-4px)

### Photo Positioning
- **Landscape photos**: Use horizontal positioning (left/right focus)
- **Portrait photos**: Use vertical positioning (top/bottom focus)
- **Square photos**: Usually center (50%, 50%) works best

### Border Styling
- **Professional**: Small border (2px), dark color (#333333)
- **Creative**: Thick border (5-8px), accent color (#3b82f6)
- **Minimal**: No border (0px)
- **Matching**: Use same color as CV accent color

### Size Guidelines
- **Small (40-60px)**: For compact CVs, sidebar position
- **Medium (60-80px)**: Default, balanced appearance
- **Large (80-120px)**: For center position, emphasis
